Select the product

Choose the product in the scroll list.

Producer

Enter the producer's name. When you are entering the name, a list appears under the confirm button. Pick the producer and click on the link.

Group interest

Enter the interest group's name. When you are entering the group, a list appears under the confirm button. Pick the interest group and click on the link.

Organization

Choose an organization among the five propositions.

Type of coffee

Choose one type of coffee among the propositions.

Batch

Enter the number of batch.

Date

Choose the date of test.

Confirm

Press the button when all fields are filled.
